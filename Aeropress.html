Coffee

Aeropress

One of the easiest and tastiest ways to make a good cup of coffee is the Aeropress. the aeropress combines immersion brewing with pourover techniques to give you the best of both styles in terms of flavor, complexity, and ease of use.

Materials and Ingredients

Kettle.

    A kitchen scale.
    Medium-fine ground coffee. Preferably freshly ground.
    An Aeropress and Aeropress plunger.
    Aeropress filter.

Instructions

  • Bring at least 250 ml of water to a boil
  • Weigh 15 grams of whole bean coffee on your scale
  • Grind your whole bean coffee to a medium-fine grind size
  • Place your Aeropress filter into your Aeropress, gently rinse the filter with water while keeping it in your Aeropress.
  • Pour your ground coffee into the 'prewet' filter and Aeropress. Gently shake the Aeropress to level the coffee bed.
  • Place your Aeropress on top of a mug so that the coffee drips into the mug
  • With boiling water, slowly pour about 30 grams of water over the coffee bed. Be sure to soak the whole bed with no dry spots.
  • After about 30 seconds, begin slowly pouring boiling water over the coffee until you reach about 250 grams of water.
  • Using the Aeropress plunger, push down gently until you reach the bottom of the Aeropress, discard used grounds and filter. The entire process should only take about 2 minutes.
  • Enjoy!
