Coffee

Aeropress

If you don't want to think too much about your coffee maker, the french press is for you. You only need a french press, some boiling water, and maybe a spoon if you want to get fancy. The coffee made in a french press has more body since it is not filtered with a paper filter. Some people prefer this while others may enjoy a "cleaner" cup of coffee.

Materials and Ingredients

Kettle.

    A kitchen scale.
    Medium ground coffee. Preferably freshly ground.
    A french press.

Instructions

  • Bring at least 500 ml of water to a boil
  • Weigh 30 grams of whole bean coffee on your scale
  • Grind your whole bean coffee to a medium grind size
  • Place your ground coffee into the french press.
  • Pour boiling water over your coffee grounds until you have 500 grams of water. Be sure to soak the whole bed of coffee.
  • Wait about 4 minutes
  • After 4 minutes, gently stir the "crust" of coffee grounds so they fall to the bottom of the french press.
  • Wait 1 minute, then slowly plunge the french press plunger down to the bottom of the french press.
  • Enjoy!
